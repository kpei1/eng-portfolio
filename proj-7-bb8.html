BB-8

BB-8’s inner electronics (left) and body (right)

  Over the summer, I designed and built a robot modeled after BB-8, a droid from the Star Wars movie series. I used what I learned throughout my freshman year at Hopkins to set up and program the robot’s electronics with Arduino. BB-8 is controlled using Bluetooth to connect to the Android app Arduino Bluetooth RC Car. I 3D modeled BB-8’s body using PTC Creo and printed it on my Prusa MK2S 3D printer. I am currently working on designing BB-8’s head.
