BB-8

BB-8’s inner electronics (left) and body (right)

  Over the summer, I designed and built a robot modeled after BB-8, a droid from the Star Wars movie series. I used what I learned throughout my freshman year at Hopkins to set up and program the robot’s electronics with Arduino. BB-8 is controlled using Bluetooth to connect to the Android app Arduino Bluetooth RC Car. I 3D modeled BB-8’s body using PTC Creo and printed it on my Prusa MK2S 3D printer.


Photo showing post to attach head (left) and completed BB-8 (right)

To attach BB-8's head, I used neodymium magnets. I mounted one magnet to a post inside BB-8's body (see photo above) and glued another to the underside of his head. The magnets were powerful enough to remain attached through the ball during testing, as can be seen in the video below.
