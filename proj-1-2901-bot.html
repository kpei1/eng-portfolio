The main objective for the 2015-2016 year was to build a robot that could climb a steep, ladder-like ramp and then hang from an upper set of bars by the end of the challenge. We designed our wheels to be able to grab and climb the rungs of the ramp. I was a member of the build team that assembled the robot. This was my first year on the robotics team, and I learned many important skills such as CAD and basic robot design. I also learned how to clearly document the design process in an engineering notebook. Our robot qualified for the state competition, and we were awarded a patent for our wheel design.
